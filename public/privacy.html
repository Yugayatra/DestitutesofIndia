Introduction

At Destitutes of India, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

a. Submitted Content

  • Photos or reports submitted by users through the "Open Camera" or upload features
  • Optional user comments, location data, or identifiers related to submissions

b. Technical Data

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, device type
  • Date and time of access
  • Cookies and usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Raise awareness by posting and showcasing submissions publicly
  • Enable NGOs or concerned authorities to locate destitute individuals and assist them
  • Improve our website and services through analytics
  • Respond to user queries or feedback

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information under:

  • Consent: When you voluntarily upload or submit data
  • Legitimate interest: To fulfill our mission of supporting destitute individuals
  • Legal obligation: If required by law enforcement or courts

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data:

  • With NGOs, social workers, or verified government partners for relief operations
  • With service providers assisting in website hosting, data analytics, or maintenance
  • If legally required by courts, government authorities, or for enforcement of terms

5. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Store preferences
  • Analyze traffic and visitor patterns
  • Enhance site speed and performance

You may disable cookies via your browser settings, but some features may not function correctly.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • As long as needed for the purposes described
  • Until you request deletion of specific submissions or data

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Ask for correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Raise a complaint with data protection authorities

For any of the above, contact: support@destitutesofindia.com

8. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with an updated effective date.
