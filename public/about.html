About Us

Our Mission

Destitutes of India is a compassionate tech-driven initiative dedicated to identifying, mapping, and aiding the destitute population across the country. Our mission is to bridge the gap between those in need and the systems that can support them — by empowering citizens to be the eyes and ears on the ground.

Making the Invisible Visible

We believe that every person deserves dignity, shelter, and care. Yet, countless individuals across India live without basic necessities, invisible to the systems meant to help them. Through our platform, users can click and upload geotagged photos of destitute individuals they encounter in public spaces. These images, along with their precise locations, are added to a growing, interactive database accessible by government bodies and verified NGOs.

Our Goal

To make the invisible visible — and reachable.

By creating a real-time, people-powered map of destitution, we aim to help authorities identify clusters, track movement patterns, and deliver timely aid such as food, shelter, medical care, and rehabilitation programs.

What Makes Us Unique

  • Citizen-Powered Data Collection: Anyone can contribute, ensuring wide coverage across urban and rural areas.
  • Geotagging for Precision: Location-based tagging enables accurate identification and quick response by nearby authorities.
  • Social Impact at Scale: With technology at the core, we aspire to scale this platform to every corner of the country.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Whether you're a concerned citizen, a social worker, or part of a government body — your involvement can change lives. One picture. One person. One action at a time.

Together, let's build a more visible, responsive, and humane India.
