About Us

YugaYatra Retail (Opc) Pvt Ltd

We are a dedicated organization committed to making a positive impact on the lives of destitutes across India. Our mission is to create awareness, provide support, and facilitate help for those in need through technology and community engagement.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between those who need help and those who can provide it, YugaYatra Retail has been working tirelessly to document and support destitutes across India. We believe that every individual deserves dignity, respect, and a chance at a better life.

Our Approach

Through our innovative platform, we enable citizens to document and share information about destitutes they encounter. This geotagged documentation helps create a comprehensive database that can be used by NGOs, government agencies, and volunteers to provide targeted assistance.

Community Impact

Our platform has already helped connect thousands of destitutes with support services, medical care, and rehabilitation programs. By leveraging technology and community participation, we're building a more compassionate and responsive society.

Privacy & Ethics

We maintain the highest standards of privacy and ethical practices. All data is collected with consent, and we ensure that the dignity and rights of individuals are always protected. Our platform operates with complete transparency and accountability.
